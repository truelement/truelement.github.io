ABOUT

TrueLement or better known as “TL” was established in 1998 at UC Berkeley. Since then, the team has evolved into a well-recognized dance group among campus peers and various communities throughout the Bay Area.

TL is known for its upbeat, diverse, and high-energy performances that highlight some of Berkeley’s most talented urban/hip-hop dancers. With such talent, TL has had the privilege to perform at many showcase venues, club events, and street festivals. TL was founded on the basis of not only promoting and performing the art of dance but also providing a unique experience for its dancers. The combination of all the dancer’s strengths, training, friendships, positivity, and love of dance creates the TL family, a very rewarding experience that can only be offered through a dance group like TL.
